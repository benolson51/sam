About

Hi, I'm Samuel.

I'm a photographer born and raised in California's Central Valley and a proud University of Oregon grad. These days you'll find me on the sidelines as a freelance photographer for Fresno State Athletics — and on weekends shooting weddings, seniors, and families all over the Valley.

Sports taught me to anticipate the moment instead of chasing it. That habit shows up in everything I photograph: I'd rather catch something real than ask you to hold a pose. Whether it's the last play of the season or the first dance, my job is to hand you back the feeling of being there.

— Samuel Marshall
